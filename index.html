"Your wolf ate your goat!"

A game based on a riddle from the 8th century.

You have three beloved belongings: a wolf, a goat, and a cabbage. You need to transport them from one side of the river to the other. You can only fit one of them at a time on your small boat.

Warning: If you leave your animals unattended...

Your wolf will eat your goat

Your goat will eat your cabbage

Use the arrow keys to move your boat left or right. Click on animals to load/unload them from your boat.

Good luck!
